About the Conference

AFEMPSC 2026 brings together researchers, faculty members, students and industry experts working in Finite Element Methods, Parallel Scientific Computing and High Performance Computing.

Conference Tracks

  • High-order FEM (hp-FEM)
  • Hybridized Discontinuous Galerkin (HDG)
  • Adaptive FEM
  • Domain Decomposition Methods
  • Parallel Iterative Solvers
  • High Performance Computing
  • Multiphysics Simulations
  • Fluid Dynamics & Plasma Physics
